Our Mission

Eradicate early-stage gastrointestinal cancers through intelligent endoluminal robotics — enabling scarless, organ-preserving removal with unprecedented precision and control.

Where we’re headed

To build the leading endoluminal robotic platform that empowers physicians to achieve surgical precision through natural orifices, making en-bloc resection of early gastrointestinal cancers faster, safer, and accessible to far more patients — while creating substantial value by converting complex open and laparoscopic procedures into scalable endoscopic ones.

Market Opportunity

A Growing Global Demand for ESD

3.4 million new GI cancer cases annually — and the only curative tool, ESD, is accessible to only a fraction of patients who need it.

Commercial Model

Capital Equipment + Recurring Consumables

Capital Equipment

System Sale — Olympus GI Pricing Parity

Priced at parity with the Olympus GI system — an established price point already in hospital procurement budgets. Lowers adoption risk and shortens sales cycles.

Recurring Revenue

Single-Use Consumables per Procedure

Each procedure consumes a single-use transport system, grasper and ESD knife — generating recurring per-procedure revenue that grows directly with case volumes.

Strategy & Structure

Hong Kong Base, Greater Bay Area Reach

Corporate Structure

Hong Kong HQ · Shenzhen Qianhai · Cayman Islands

Headquartered in Hong Kong, with a Shenzhen Qianhai office for GBA clinical collaboration and manufacturing. The Hong Kong entity is 100% owned by a Cayman Islands holding structure — enabling clean international investment and capital markets access.

GBA Advantage

Access to China's Largest ESD Market

Direct access to mainland China's hospital network — the world's largest ESD market. Supports NMPA regulatory engagement and commercial partnerships with Chinese hospital groups.

Regulatory & Policy Tailwinds

Healthy China 2030 & Innovation Policy

  • ► Healthy China 2030 — national initiative targeting reduced GI cancer mortality through early detection, directly driving ESD volumes and government investment in advanced platforms.
  • ► Medical Robotics Policy — preferential procurement for innovative robotic platforms in public hospitals.
  • ► NMPA Innovative Device Designation — fast-track pathway significantly reduces regulatory timelines for breakthrough robotics platforms.
