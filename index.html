Our Mission

Eradicate early-stage gastrointestinal cancers through intelligent endoluminal robotics — enabling scarless, organ-preserving removal with unprecedented precision and control.

Where we’re headed

To build the leading endoluminal robotic platform that empowers physicians to achieve surgical precision through natural orifices, making en-bloc resection of early gastrointestinal cancers faster, safer, and accessible to far more patients — while creating substantial value by converting complex open and laparoscopic procedures into scalable endoscopic ones.
