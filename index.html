System Design

Three Key Innovations,
One Integrated Platform

Enhanced Vision

Bird's-eye all-in-one camera provides a clear, stable view of the surgical field — eliminating the visual limitations of conventional single-channel endoscopy during ESD.

Robotic Traction

A dedicated robotic Lifter arm provides precise, adjustable tissue traction — the critical missing element that slows ESD and causes perforation risk.

Precise Dissection

A multi-functional Dissector arm with a novel ESD knife executes clean, controlled submucosal dissection — reducing procedure time and complication risk.

How It Works

Solving the Three Core Challenges of ESD

01

Vision

The all-in-one 14mm endoscopic platform provides a bird's-eye view of the operative field. The camera is decoupled from the instruments — delivering stable, unobstructed intraoperative vision throughout the procedure.

02

Traction

The robotic Lifter arm grips and elevates the tissue flap with consistent, adjustable tension — creating the submucosal working space that ESD demands without requiring an assistant or clip-and-line techniques.

03

Dissection

The Dissector arm, equipped with a patented multi-functional ESD knife, performs the submucosal dissection with smooth, tremor-free movements — achieving dissection speeds matching those of expert endoscopists (~170 mm²/min).

04

Master Console Control

The surgeon operates both robotic arms simultaneously from an ergonomic master console — maintaining full control and reducing the physical and cognitive load of conventional ESD.
